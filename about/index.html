Cipher Challenge 2025 - Cryptography Edition

The UM6P College of Computing is pleased to invite students from across Morocco to take part in the Cipher Challenge 2025, a competition designed to test and celebrate cryptographic skills. Hosted on the UM6P Rabat campus, this event brings together the brightest minds to engage in an exciting intellectual challenge, with prizes rewarding outstanding performances.

EVENT DETAILS

Date: Saturday, April 12, and Sunday, April 13, 2025

Location: UM6P College of Computing – Rabat Campus

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

Students under 22 years of age (by December 31st, 2025) enrolled in Bac+2/Bac+3 scientific programs:

  • Preparatory Classes (CPGE)
  • Bachelor’s Programs (DEUG, Licence, etc.)

Participation is individual. Limited spots available.

HOW TO JOIN?

  • Please fill out the form to express your interest: https://lnkd.in/e_jCnU4g
  • Deadline to apply: Sunday, February 23, 2025

For more information or questions, contact us at: cc-cipherchallenge@um6p.ma

We look forward to welcoming you to the Cipher Challenge 2025.

About the UM6P College of Computing:

The UM6P College of Computing, located at University Mohammed VI Polytechnic Campuses in Benguerir and in Rabat, is a leading institution in Computer Science education and research. Led by world-class faculty, the college is dedicated to advancing computer science knowledge, driving innovation, and preparing the next generation of tech leaders to address global challenges through technology.
